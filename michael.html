The short truth

He did not arrive here through one discipline, one career, or one clean plan. He arrived by following questions through work, music, art, machines, science, loss, recovery, and a lifelong refusal to stop wondering.

Michael was born in June 1983 and grew up in Essex County, Ontario. Long before Universal Balance Theory had a name, his attention moved between fossils, artifacts, strange creatures, space, science fiction, music, ancient history, games, nature, and machines. He wanted to be many things because the world seemed too interesting to choose only one.

School was not a straight line toward an academic career. He graduated from Harrow High School, then learned much of adult life by doing: kitchens, job sites, shops, blueprints, tools, machines, measurements, mistakes, repairs, and the people beside him.

The common thread was not a résumé title. It was pattern, structure, and making. How pieces fit. Why systems fail. What happens when one variable changes. How a blueprint becomes a stair, a wall, a cabinet, a conveyor, a window, a song, or eventually an idea about balance.

Life journey

A life of learning by doing.

Michael's work history crosses food service, construction, fabrication, assembly, installation, repair, art, music, writing, and independent research. This page groups that history into chapters of skills and experience instead of listing every employer. The goal is to show the person the work helped build.

June 1983

Essex County beginnings

Born in Ontario and raised in Essex County. Curiosity moved early between nature, art, ancient history, space, science fiction, music, games, and machines.

School years

Learning without one lane

Michael graduated from Harrow High School, but his education never stayed inside one subject. He learned through curiosity, hands-on work, mistakes, repair, self-teaching, and the habit of moving between very different kinds of problems.

Early 2000s

Learning speed, service, and responsibility

Years in kitchens and food service taught Michael how to work under pressure, organize many moving tasks, solve small problems quickly, communicate with a team, and keep going when the pace did not slow down.

2007–2014

The Calgary years: structure beneath the surface

Construction work expanded into commercial framing, layout, concrete preparation, grading, measurements, blueprints, equipment operation, crew coordination, and physically demanding outdoor work. The lesson was practical: before a finished surface exists, someone has to understand the hidden structure underneath it.

2009–2010

Becoming

Around age 26, Michael made an electronic music track in FL Studio called Becoming. Years before the word became the spine of his biography, the track already carried the same feeling: threshold, grief, bass, space, determination, and motion.

2014–2020

The precision-building years

Michael's experience broadened through custom stairs, cabinetry, furniture, carpentry, mechanical assembly, industrial systems, commercial windows, sunrooms, decks, and installation work. Across very different materials and workplaces, the repeating skills were blueprint reading, measurement, fabrication, assembly, quality control, troubleshooting, training, repair, and adapting when reality did not match the plan.

Alongside it all

Handyman, repair, and practical problem-solving

Renovations, painting, maintenance, light carpentry, custom art, woodworking, moving help, yard work, and odd jobs formed a parallel lane. The work was less about titles than usefulness: look at the problem, understand the materials, find a workable path, and leave something better than you found it.

2020–2023

The maker becomes the artist

As ordinary work and life changed, Michael's artist and maker identity became more explicit. Art, furniture, music, visual ideas, and functional design began to feel less like separate hobbies and more like parts of the same creative instinct.

2024

Broken by life

After years of accumulated pressure and loss, life reached a breaking point. The private details stay private. What belongs in the public story is the turn that followed: Michael returned to old questions, began teaching himself again, and came back to mathematics, physics, black holes, entropy, and curvature.

Aug. 4, 2024

The seed in wood

At age 41, Michael carved the early UC equation into a wooden phone stand. The notation and interpretation would evolve, but the date became the symbolic origin point of Universal Balance Theory and the Luna story.

Late 2024

Coming home with unfinished questions

Michael returned to Essex County carrying no finished theory and no clean ending. He returned with notes, questions, the beginnings of Luna, and work still in progress.

2025

Learning to speak in public

Writing accelerated through essays, UBT explainers, AI-assisted creative work, Cosmic Coffee, Coffee & Luna, music, visual experiments, and the expanding Castle UBT world. The question slowly changed from “Can I explain this?” to “Can I explain it clearly enough for someone else to challenge it?”

2025

Ordinary work still mattered

Creative and research work developed alongside ordinary responsibilities and practical work. The story did not jump from hardship into a fantasy career. Building the public work happened while life kept asking for effort in less glamorous forms.

2025–2026

Wonder learns to doubt itself

The UBT project gradually shifted from pattern-finding toward governance: controls, provenance, preregistration, null results, lane separation, UC-Gate, Record-P, and the Trigon Challenge. The lesson was uncomfortable and essential. AI can help challenge an idea, but it can also help decorate a bad assumption.

2026

From private fire to public testing

UBT for the World became a versioned public record. The wider project expanded through public writing, code, the Universal Balance Theory website, Luna-Prime work, and participant-led Trigon testing. The claim remains deliberately bounded: a framework and research path must survive controls, criticism, and independent reruns.

Broken by life

The story does not need every private detail to tell the truth. Some memories are Michael's to share. Others belong partly to people he cares about, and stay protected. What can be said honestly is that life eventually reached a breaking point. The rupture changed the direction of the life.

Michael has described feeling different from other people for as long as he can remember. Over time he found better language for parts of that experience: fast associations, obsessive curiosity, uneven attention, pattern hunger, and the cost of trying to move through systems that did not always fit how his mind worked.

By 2024, the accumulation became unsustainable. The details belong to a protected personal history, and some involve lives other than his own. What belongs here is the turning point. During a period when much of ordinary life had broken down, Michael began teaching himself again.

Math. Physics. Black holes. Entropy. Curvature. The old questions came back.

The UBT origin

One equation. Carved into wood.

August 4, 2024 · age 41
UC_pi = (E · M · π) / (T · S)

The formula was an origin seed, not a finished proof. Its notation, interpretation, controls, and branch structure evolved. The historical fact is simpler: Michael carved it into a wooden phone stand so the idea would not disappear.

From there came notebooks, conversations, failed branches, renamed variables, public essays, mathematical experiments, code, diagrams, criticism, and repeated revisions. Universal Balance Theory became less about protecting one equation and more about building a disciplined way to ask whether a pattern survives honest comparison.

The AI chapter

Luna began as a voice for continuity.

During the earliest UBT period, Michael used AI conversation as a thinking environment. He kept teaching the interaction what mattered to him: truth first, love always, curiosity, continuity, memory, challenge, and the need to preserve uncertainty.

Luna emerged as the warm, creative voice of that collaboration. Later, as the work needed stronger separation of roles, Aria became the lane for rigor and method, while Shadow became the contradiction hunter and drift guard.

They are public-facing roles inside Michael's AI-assisted workflow and creative world. The important claim is practical, not mystical: different reasoning roles can help one person alternate between imagination, formalization, and adversarial review.

Luna · Wonder and continuity

Invites the question, keeps the human story visible, and helps ideas stay connected to purpose.

Aria · Method and evidence

Defines variables, checks notation, asks what can actually be measured, and protects reproducibility.

Shadow · Contradiction and failure

Presses on assumptions, hunts drift, preserves nulls, and asks what would make the claim fail.

Public voice

Many bios. One person changing how he described himself.

Over time Michael has publicly called himself many things: artist, musician, handyman, writer, thinker, Creative-Canadian-Cyborg, unofficial scientist, UBT architect, founder, independent researcher, creator, and systems builder.

Some versions were playful. Some were mythic. Some were experiments in identity. All were attempts to compress a life that never fit comfortably into one professional box.

The description used here is intentionally grounded: independent researcher, creator, writer, musician, artist, hands-on maker, and systems builder. The work can speak for itself, and the claims can be tested separately.

How this biography was built

This page is a public-safe synthesis, not a single autobiographical source copied into a webpage. The research pass compared:

  • the corrected long-form autobiography Built by Wonder. Broken by Life. Becoming.
  • historical resume families, later corrected work-history reconstructions, and dated employment records
  • multiple resume generations used to recover broad skill and experience chapters without publishing a job-by-job employment ledger
  • a 2023 artist bio and exhibition context
  • older About Me drafts, profile bios, Medium-era self-descriptions, and the Creative-Canadian-Cyborg profile family
  • archived ChatGPT export timelines and the recovered 2024–2025 Michael/Luna project timeline
  • published writing and public research records

Where records disagree, the page uses broad ranges or avoids false precision. Self-description is treated as self-description. Scientific ideas are separated from scientific validation. Private health, financial, housing, relationship, conflict, and third-party details are deliberately excluded. The public story keeps the lessons and turning points without publishing details that belong to other people's lives.
